    # Services

    • Predictive Business Analytics
      Analyze business data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities. Transform raw data into actionable insights for operational and strategic decisions.

    • Time Series Forecasting
      Build reliable forecasts for demand, prices, KPIs, or operational metrics. Techniques include ARIMA, LSTM, and hybrid models, tailored to your business context.

    • Decision Support & Scenario Analysis
      Model and evaluate “what-if” scenarios to guide strategic and operational choices. Includes KPI-driven evaluation and uncertainty analysis.

    • Initial Analytics Workshop
      1–2 day workshop to define forecasting goals, assess data quality, and outline a concrete analytics roadmap. Ideal for clients starting with data-driven decision-making.
