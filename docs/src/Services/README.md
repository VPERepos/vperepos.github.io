# Services

- <strong>Predictive Business Analytics</strong> <br/>
Analyze business data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities.
Transform raw data into actionable insights for operational and strategic decisions.

- <strong>Time Series Forecasting</strong> <br/>
Build reliable forecasts for demand, prices, KPIs, or operational metrics.
Techniques include ARIMA, LSTM, and hybrid models, tailored to your business context.

- <strong>Decision Support & Scenario Analysis</strong> <br/>
Model and evaluate “what-if” scenarios to guide strategic and operational choices.
Includes KPI-driven evaluation and uncertainty analysis.

- <strong>Initial Analytics Workshop</strong> <br/>
1–2 day workshop to define forecasting goals, assess data quality, and outline a concrete analytics roadmap.
Ideal for clients starting with data-driven decision-making.
